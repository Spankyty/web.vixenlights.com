{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead color="primary" %}} Vixen helps you to create amazing syncronized light shows for any holiday, event, or experience you can dream up. Teamed with readily available lights and hardware, Vixen supports everything you need to harness your creativity and syncronize your lights to music. These shows are typlically seen at Christmas, but they have grown in popularity to other holidays and events.
